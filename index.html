Course Description

The course is an introduction to Linux operating system. The course will discuss introductory concepts of linux operating systems and will have hand's on session to make students comfortable with unix based operating systems. We will In'Sha Allah cover the basics of estbalishing services on linux (perferably ubuntu). Establishing web servers, DNS, FTP, SSH, DHCP etc., will be focus of the course.

Pre Requiste

Students are expected to be comfortable with computers. Though the relevant topics will be discussed as and when required but students are expected to follow the topics for smooth execution of class.
